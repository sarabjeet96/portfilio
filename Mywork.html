Car Pool

Car Pool is a web based project that provides various information about car owners and their cars so that people can pool with others going for same path or destination. Car pooling or ride-sharing is a sharing of car journeys so that people going to similar location can get to that place more conveniently without the need of driving the vehicle by themselves. Car pooling is also a better way of utilizing the full seat capacity of the car.Some Benefits of car pooling areas follows Reduce your carbon footprint Carpooling reduces that amount significantly. If you carpool from home and to work, you can probably reduce your transportation emissions number in half, or even better.
